# 02. Vision

## 1. Purpose
This document defines the overarching "Why" behind the Financial Literacy Simulator (FLS). It aligns the engineering execution with the long-term goals of the National Centre for Financial Education (NCFE) and the National Strategy for Financial Education (NSFE 3.0).

## 2. Scope
The vision serves as the ultimate guiding principle for all architectural and design decisions. If a proposed feature does not serve this vision, it must be discarded or deprioritized.

## 3. The Core Vision
**"To create a financially aware and empowered India through consequence-driven experiential learning."**

Traditional financial education relies heavily on passive textbook learning, which fails to prepare individuals for the emotional stress of real-world financial crises. The FLS aims to act as a **Digital Public Good**, providing a sandbox where citizens can experience the pain of high-interest debt or the joy of compounding returns in a simulated environment, rather than learning these lessons through real-world bankruptcy.

## 4. Alignment with NSFE 3.0 (The '5 C' Approach)
* **Content:** Shifting from curriculum to interactive "Sandboxes".
* **Capacity:** Providing educators with tech tools rather than pamphlets.
* **Community:** Open-source localization allowing states to fork and modify tax/subsidy logic.
* **Communication:** Gamifying finance to speak the language of Gen Z.
* **Collaboration:** Bridging regulators with the massive Open-Source Software (OSS) ecosystem.

## 5. Product Philosophy (Self-Determination Theory)
We explicitly reject superficial gamification (Points, Badges, Leaderboards). These trigger the *Over-Justification Effect*, destroying long-term engagement.
Instead, our engineering must support **Intrinsic Mastery**:
* **Autonomy:** No forced linear tutorials. Players choose their own path.
* **Competence:** Clear, visual dashboards showing Net Worth growth over decades.
* **Relatedness:** (Post-MVP) Engaging with simulated families and peer cohorts.

## 7. Future Considerations
The ultimate vision is a fully dynamic, multi-generational simulation where players not only manage their own wealth but navigate complex, AI-driven macroeconomic cycles and form cooperative financial syndicates. These remain Post-Internship goals.
