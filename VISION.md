# Project Vision

## Why This Project Exists
Financial literacy is not universally taught, yet it universally impacts human well-being. Millions of people make critical life choices—taking out student loans, buying a home, investing for retirement—without fully understanding the long-term compounding effects of those decisions.

The cost of learning through real-world mistakes is often decades of financial hardship.

## Our Approach
We believe the most effective way to learn is through experience. By creating an interactive, realistic life simulator, we can provide a safe sandbox for people to experiment with financial strategies. 

Users can experience decades of financial decisions in minutes through realistic simulations. They can see what happens if they invest early, take on high-interest debt, or neglect their emergency fund.

## The Ultimate Goal
To become the global standard for accessible, open-source financial education. We envision this tool being used by individuals for personal growth, by teachers in classrooms, and by organizations aiming to uplift their communities.\n
