# Project Governance

This document describes how the Financial Literacy Simulator project is governed.

## Roles and Responsibilities

### Contributors
Anyone who contributes to the project, whether through code, documentation, design, or research. Contributors are the lifeblood of the project.

### Triagers
Contributors who have shown a commitment to the project and are granted permissions to manage issues and pull requests (labeling, closing, assigning).

### Maintainers
Individuals with write access to the repository. They are responsible for reviewing and merging pull requests, steering the technical direction, and ensuring the project adheres to its vision.

### Core Team
A small group of highly active maintainers who make final decisions on architecture, roadmap, and project governance.

## Decision Making
We aim for consensus-driven decision making. For major architectural or design changes, a proposal should be submitted (via an Issue or Discussion) and discussed by the community. If consensus cannot be reached, the Core Team will make the final decision.\n
