# Financial Literacy Simulator (India) 🇮🇳

[![Open Source](https://badges.frapsoft.com/os/v1/open-source.svg?v=103)](https://opensource.org/)
[![Status: Research & Design](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Research_%26_Design-blue)](#roadmap)

An open-source, gamified financial life simulation engine designed to align with the **National Centre for Financial Education's (NCFE)** upcoming **National Strategy for Financial Education (NSFE 3.0)**. 

## 🇮🇳 The Vision: A Digital Public Good for India
India has one of the largest youth populations in the world, yet traditional financial education relies heavily on passive textbook learning. 

We are building this simulator as a **Digital Public Good (DPG)**. The goal is to provide a highly realistic, interactive sandbox where Indian citizens can experience 50 years of financial decisions in 30 minutes. By learning through simulated consequences (e.g., taking high-interest loans, neglecting health insurance, delaying SIP investments), we aim to create a financially aware and empowered India.

## 🎯 Alignment with NSFE 3.0 (The '5 C' Approach)
This project is being architected specifically to support the Government of India and Financial Sector Regulators (RBI, SEBI, IRDAI, PFRDA).
- **Content:** Shifting from theoretical curriculum to experiential "Sandboxes."
- **Capacity:** Empowering educators with tech tools for interactive workshops.
- **Community:** Open-source architecture allowing local developers to add state-specific subsidies and realities.
- **Communication:** Gamifying finance to speak the language of Gen Z and Millennials.
- **Collaboration:** Bridging the gap between regulators and India's massive Open-Source Software (OSS) developer ecosystem.

*(Read our official proposal draft to the NCFE [here](docs/research/NCFE_NSFE_3.0_Proposal.md))*

## 🚀 Project Goals
- **Hyper-Realistic Indian Context:** Accurately model Indian financial systems, including Income Tax slabs (Old vs New regime), EPF/PPF, Mutual Funds (SIPs), inflation, and localized real estate markets.
- **Accessibility:** Create an intuitive, mobile-first interface accessible to users across different demographics.
- **Extensibility:** Build a modular open-source engine where contributors can easily add new localized financial events.

## 🗺️ Roadmap
We are currently in the early stages of building our foundation. We have opened **80 research and design issues** to crowdsource the best financial math and UX paradigms. 
- **Phase 1: Research** (Current Phase - Defining formulas, tax brackets, and events)
- **Phase 2: Design** (UI/UX wireframes for the dashboard)
- **Phase 3: MVP** (Core simulation engine with basic Indian scenarios)
- **Phase 4: Alpha** (Graphical interface and complex instruments like Home Loans)
- **Phase 5: Beta** (Public testing and localization)
- **Phase 6: Stable 1.0** (Official release)
