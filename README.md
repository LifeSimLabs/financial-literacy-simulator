# Financial Literacy Simulator

## Vision
An open-source financial life simulation platform where users experience decades of financial decisions in minutes through realistic simulations. The goal is to improve financial literacy by learning through consequences instead of lectures.

## Mission
To provide accessible, engaging, and realistic financial education to everyone, everywhere, for free. We believe that financial literacy is a fundamental life skill that should not be gatekept by expensive courses or complex jargon.

## The Problem
Many people enter adulthood without a basic understanding of personal finance. Traditional financial education often relies on dry lectures or abstract concepts. When people learn through trial and error in the real world, the mistakes can be devastating and take years to recover from.

## The Solution
A realistic, interactive simulator where users can make financial decisions—budgeting, investing, taking on debt, buying a home, or changing careers—and instantly see the long-term consequences. By compressing decades of financial life into minutes, we enable experiential learning without real-world risk.

## Project Goals
- **Realism:** Accurately model financial systems, including compound interest, taxes, inflation, and market volatility.
- **Accessibility:** Create an intuitive, engaging user interface that doesn't require a finance degree to understand.
- **Extensibility:** Build a modular system where contributors can add new financial events, scenarios, and localized economic conditions.
